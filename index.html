Hi, I’m Mano.

Builder, Engineer, Researcher, Leader — and Father. All in one package.

I'm a VP at Meta, leading the Media Applied Research organization within Meta SuperIntelligence Labs (MSL). My group focuses on multimodal understanding and generation — pushing the frontier and shipping capabilities used by billions of people every day.

My Meta journey started as an intern in 2012. The passion has been constant: keep pushing AI capabilities — early on in Computer Vision, now across modalities — with an eye to scale and accessibility for everyone. Lately, I'm excited to build multimodal agents that help people create, collaborate, and connect.

A few things about me

  • I grew up in India and live in the Bay Area.
